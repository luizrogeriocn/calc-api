calc-api
========

Calculator API.

## Services
### Calculations
  - Add two numbers
    - ```POST to add/:numA:numB```
  - Subtract two numbers
     - ```POST to sub/:numA:numB```
  - Multiply two numbers
    - ```POST to mult/:numA:numB```
  - Divide two numbers
    - ```POST to div/:numA:numB```
